You can try these simple daily challenges

  1. Use a reusable water bottle.
  2. Keep a green bad handy.
  3. Use aluminium foil instead of cling film.
  4. Decline plastic straws.
  5. Bring your reusable cup.
  6. Compost food waste.
  7. Refuse plastic cutlery.
  8. Use a disposable handkerchief instead of disposable tissues.
  9. Switch to e-bills.
  10. Reuse jars and containers for leftovers.
  11. Only choose snacks without plastic packaging.
  12. Bring your own bags for fresh produce at the supermarket.
  13. Try a solid bar soap instead of plastic bottles.
  14. Buy natural fabrics not synthetic.
  15. Drink your coffee in the cafe if you forget you cup.
